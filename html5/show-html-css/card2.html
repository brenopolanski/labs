<h3 class="card-title">

Golden lion tamarin


</h3>

<div class="card-picture"> </div>

<div class="card-content">
<p class="card-info text-italic">

The golden lion tamarin is an endangered species with an estimated wild population of about 3,200 individuals spread between four places along southeastern Brazil, and a captive population maintained at about 490 individuals among 150 zoos.

</p>
    <ul class="card-details">


  • <li class="item"> Scientific Name: Leontopithecus rosalia </li>


    • </ul>
<p class="card-info">

The golden lion tamarin gets its name from its bright reddish orange pelage and the extra long hairs around the face and ears which give it a distinctive mane. Its face is dark and hairless.

</p>
